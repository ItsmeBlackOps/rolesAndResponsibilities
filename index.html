Silverspace SOP: Roles & Responsibilities

⚠️ Accountability Warning: Any deviation from this checklist—especially missing MANDATORY items—makes the expert fully responsible if a complaint arises.

Expert

  • Create end-to-end project docs, conduct prep calls, draft breakdowns & storylines, schedule mocks (Resume Understanding).
  • Acknowledge support requests immediately, manage 30-20-10 check-ins, live-update answers, share support docs (Screening).
  • Guide technical/final round answers naturally, verify audio/screen share, explain algorithms & complexities, log examples (Final Round).
  • Set up assessments, obtain access, record sessions, comment on syntax & share real-time feedback (Assessment Support).
  • Respond to emails, create invites with proper CCs, attach templates, and close tickets with full notes (Email/Calendar).
  • Failure Accountability: Expert is responsible for any SOP deviations in their own tasks.

Team Lead

  • Manage Experts: audit their SOP adherence at every phase and coach them on mandatory/optional checklist items.
  • Provide Experts with templates, assets & cheat-sheets; ensure all “Before/During/After” tasks appear in shared Google Docs.
  • Monitor Experts’ KPIs, flag delays or missing actions, and escalate deviations to the Assistant Manager.
  • Consolidate session logs & recordings; initiate Root Cause Analysis on any SOP breach.
  • Track candidate comfort & document quality; approve final docs before sharing with candidates.
  • Failure Accountability: Team Lead is responsible for any oversight or coaching failures.

Assistant Manager

  • Manage Team Leads: review audits, verify escalations, and ping for non-compliance within defined SLAs.
  • Produce daily/weekly/monthly KPI reports on Expert & Team Lead performance, SOP adherence, and outcomes.
  • Lead Root Cause Analysis workshops with Team Leads; define corrective actions and update process docs.
  • Oversee interview scheduling: send acknowledgments, calendar invites, and distribute SOP checklists.
  • Maintain & curate the Template Drop Zone: Doc templates, Q&A sheets, behavior guides, and feedback formats.
  • Failure Accountability: Assistant Manager is responsible for missed escalations or reporting gaps.

Manager

  • Define & refine KPIs measuring compliance and performance at every level: Expert, Team Lead, Assistant Manager.
  • Assign Experts, Team Leads & Assistant Manager based on expertise, workload, and candidate volume.
  • Enforce Zero-Tolerance Policy: track infractions, issue warnings, and escalate or remove non-compliant participants.
  • Review escalations from the Assistant Manager; ensure fairness and consistency in applying consequences.
  • Audit & update the master SOP annually (or as needed); communicate changes and train the team.
  • Failure Accountability: Manager holds overall responsibility for any systemic or policy-level failures.
